What this is

{{ settings.site_name }} pulls opportunities (funding, calls for papers, conferences) and research items from RSS/Atom feeds and tags them by region. It also exposes a weekly RSS digest that you can plug into an RSS newsletter in MailerLite.

How the free newsletter works
  1. Run the app (Docker Compose).
  2. Open /admin/ingest once (or let the worker do it) to populate items.
  3. In MailerLite, create an RSS campaign that points to {{ settings.public_base_url }}/feeds/newsletter.xml.
  4. Mailing lists are automatically created per region when users subscribe on the site.

Note: MailerLite's API lets us add subscribers and manage groups, but building/sending a fully-designed email via API may depend on your plan. RSS campaigns are the most reliable free approach.
